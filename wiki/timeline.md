# Timeline — Life, Career, and the Blog's Arc

Dates are absolute. Biographical facts are drawn from the blog itself (he keeps his private
life mostly off it on purpose) plus widely known public record. Career detail he states
directly is cited to post slugs.

## Pre-blog (background)

- **Born Oct 2, 1989** (the blog's "Happy Birthday to Me," 2022, is his 33rd).
- **2007 (age 17):** first to unlock/jailbreak the iPhone. **2010:** jailbroke the PS3 →
  Sony lawsuit. Origin of his hacker reputation and the "who has root?" ethos. See
  [[hacking-and-jailbreaks]].
- **Internships & short stints:** 3 internships at Google (two at 18/19; one writing the
  open-source [[hacking-and-jailbreaks|qira]]); ~9 months at **Facebook in 2011**, quit before
  shares vested because "wasting the world's time" was a dumb mission; later **~5 weeks at
  Twitter** (post-Musk acquisition) hoping it could be different ([[how-do-i-stop]],
  [[what-is-possible]]). He stresses he has **never made money from big tech** — his money came
  from pwn2own, CTFs, crypto contracting (Optimism's Cannon), and not spending money.
- **2015:** founds **comma.ai** (self-driving). See [[comma-ai]].
- **Oct 17, 2020:** first commit to **tinygrad** (started as a toy to learn neural nets).

## 2020–2021 — the worldview forms (on the blog)

The bedrock. Posts establish: the master frame ([[technology-without-industry]]), the
money/value theory ([[on-money-creation]]), the anti-[[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking|PMC]] thesis
([[class-warfare]], [[pieces-of-shit]]), neoreaction/regime analysis ([[the-fourth-estate]]),
AI as machine life that fights entropy ([[a-machine-ecology]], [[to-fight-against-entropy]]),
[[power-centralization|individual sovereignty]] (bought 123 acres in Lakeside CA, named it
*ecromata*; later shelved), and the AI-chip thesis with a famous public reversal on VLIW
([[hacking-and-jailbreaks|a-breakdown-of-ai-chip-companies]] → a-correction-on-ai-chips).

## 2022 — exit, and the seed of tiny corp

Takes time away from comma ([[the-heroes-journey]]) — "it's no longer a race car, it's a
boat." His Twitter stint ([[eacc]]) crystallizes a "new theory of management" (chaos prevents
process; replace the dictator with AI). First names **"the Tiny Corporation."** Cultural
pieces sharpen: [[consumer-class-divide]], [[the-problem-of-the-state]] (exit vs. voice),
[[wireheading-and-moloch|beauty-after-covid]]. Movie-idea streak begins (Avatar 2 rewrite).

## 2023 — the tiny corp is real

**May 24, 2023:** the tiny corp raises **$5.1M** ([[the-tiny-corp-raised-5M]]); mission =
"commoditize the petaflop." Public war with AMD's broken ROCm drivers begins. Key AI essays:
[[ai-and-agi|there-is-no-hard-takeoff]] (no FOOM), debates **Yudkowsky** on [[ai-and-agi|p(doom)]].
Political arc deepens: [[wireheading-and-moloch|wireheading-city]], [[government-and-society|three-class-society]],
[[government-and-society|disrupt-the-government]]. comma's imitation-learning lineage explained.

## 2024 — China, Elon, and the move

Spends months in Asia (mostly Hong Kong); contrasts US decline with [[government-and-society|high-trust society]].
Backs **Trump/Elon 2024** as an "Elon swing voter" while flagging fiscal/protectionist
concerns. "Bitter lesson stops at human DNA" ([[ai-and-agi|where-the-bitter-lesson-ends]]),
[[wireheading-and-moloch|influence-agents]], "money is the map," and [[free-will-and-the-soul|the-soul]]
("you are a machine learning algorithm"). Decides to leave California, looks at HK residency.

## 2025 — Hong Kong resident, tinygrad maturing

Becomes a **Hong Kong resident** ([[gambling-is-bad]]). [[money-is-the-map]] and "produce more
than you consume" ([[you-are-a-good-person]]) become his economic core. **AMD YOLO** (MI300X
boxes arrive; buys AMD stock). "[[ai-and-agi|Can tinygrad win?]]" + the **AMD contract** to
get MLPerf LLaMA-405B training competitive with NVIDIA. Writes a 7-chapter **Black-Mirror-style
fiction series** ("chapter one: john" … "chapter seven: nudge") dramatizing determinism/nudge.
[[ai-and-agi|AI Coding]]: "the best model of a programming AI is a compiler." **Dec 2025:**
Opus 4.5 changes his mind — "the first model that can use computers at all" ([[computer-use-models]]).
**Five years of tinygrad:** ~18,935 lines, 6 people, ~$2M/yr from tinybox sales.

## 2026 (through May) — neofeudalism, UBI reversal, "it's just movement"

The current George. Dominant themes: **closed AI = neofeudalism / "who has root?"**
([[free-intelligence]], [[three-minutes]], [[us-win-ai]]); the **application layer is worthless**,
Chinese open models (Kimi/Qwen/GLM) are the good guys ([[the-last-gasps-of-the-rent-seeking-class]],
[[ai-has-no-moat]]); **UBI is insane** → then the sarcastic **reversal** ("the only way to kill
Social Security is to print worthless money," [[changing-my-mind-on-ubi]]); **America lost the
Mandate of Heaven** + **Five Simple Steps to Fix America**; **the tiny corp's product is a
training box that *learns* per-user** ([[tiny-corp-product]]); and the mature AI take —
**there is no singularity, only movement; the one bad scenario is a singleton**
([[real-singularity]], [[one-bad-scenario]], [[the-importance-of-diversity]]). Stops streaming
([[punk-or-why-i-dont-stream]]). Latest: **The Eternal Sloptember** — AI agents can't really
program; a golden era of slop, a dark age for quality.
