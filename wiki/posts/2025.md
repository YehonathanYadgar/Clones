# Post summaries — 2025 (Hong Kong, "produce more than you consume", tinygrad matures)

  technocratic plod"; real Type-2 experiences are vanishing. "Spiritual tops vs bottoms"; give
  control of tech to as many people as possible "in a nature sense, not a permissions sense."
  [[wireheading-and-moloch]].
- **Nobody Profits** (`nobody-will-profit`, 2025-02-19) — IP is dumb (piracy ≠ theft). "The best
  outcome of AI is huge *value* and no *profit*." Open source to "market break everything."

- **Money is the Map** (`money-is-the-map`, 2025-02-24) — The load-bearing economics essay. Money
  represents value; a map detached from territory dies. "From each according to his ability, to
  each according to his ability to effectively deploy capital." [[economics-and-value]].

  ~$250k AMD stock. "CUDA isn't really the moat." [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].

  relative. His grim 5-step model: indulged resentment → purges → real poverty → healing. "The
  demoralization is just beginning." [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]].
