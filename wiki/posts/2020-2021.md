# Post summaries — 2020–2021 (the worldview forms)

One entry per blog post. Slug in `()`. These are faithful, citeable summaries; the synthesis
lives in [[overview]] and the [[topics]] pages.

## 2020

- **On Money Creation** (`on-money-creation`, 2020-08-07) — His first post. Three job groups:
  constant (parasitic service/movers), linear (farmers/miners/manufacturers — the real base),
  exponential (scientists/engineers). US is ~73% "group 1"; a group-1-only economy goes broke
  like a ghost town. Seeds [[economics-and-value]] and [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]].
- **A Silicon Person** (`a-silicon-person`, 2020-08-20) — Estimates the brain as ~20 PFLOPS /
  200TB of weights; prices a brain-scale computer at ~$20M + $1600/hr. Begins his "person of
  compute" thread. [[ai-and-agi]].

## 2021

- **Technology without Industry** (`technology-without-industry`, 2021-01-18) — The master-axis
  post. "Does this centralize or decentralize power?" Waymo (brittle, needs society) vs comma
  (self-contained). "STOP BUILDING SHITTY TECHNOLOGY." Biology as the model. [[power-centralization]].
- **The AI Control Problem** (`the-ai-control-problem`, 2021-02-28) — Alignment is a problem of
  *Power*, not math; "rationalist hogwash." Superintelligence ≠ supermorality. Edgy, NRx-adjacent.
  [[free-will-and-the-soul]], [[ai-and-agi]].
- **Sell Outs** (`sell-outs`, 2021-04-20) — Punk lyrics → "YOU ARE PART OF THE SYSTEM… underpaid
  research divisions of big tech." Disdain for VC-lesson-plan founders. "It's all optimization
  landscapes." [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]].
- **A Machine Ecology** (`a-machine-ecology`, 2021-04-25) — Tech without humans "looks pathetic"
  (his 123 acres decaying). The real goal of AI: machines that fight entropy, reproduce, sustain
  — a "silicon ecology." The Coffee Test > self-driving. [[comma-ai]], [[ai-and-agi]].
- **Idiots are going to kill me** (`idiots-are-going-to-kill-me`, 2021-05-07) — COVID lab-leak
  musing; gain-of-function vs banned human-cloning/intelligence research. "The minimum IQ to
  destroy the world drops by one point every 18 months."
- **A Circulation of the Elites** (`a-circulation-of-the-elites`, 2021-06-10) — Annotates his own
  2020 rap. The current elites are cowards who'll flee before the crash; "I dream of a world
  where we fight for power over nature instead of power over people." [[persona]].
- **A Breakdown of AI Chip Companies** (`a-breakdown-of-ai-chip-companies`, 2021-06-13) — Savage
  teardown of Graphcore/Groq/Cerebras/etc. ("secrecy is cover for 'we don't have shit'"). VLIW &
  big chips = bad. Lays out the "Cherry Computer" plan. Later reversed → see below. [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **Pieces of Shit** (`pieces-of-shit`, 2021-06-23) — "Capitalism is so rigged it isn't fun
  anymore." 3 policy darts (500% ad tax, 1% trade tax, separate church/state incl. Harvard).
  "The only real solution is shame." [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]].
- **Baskets** (`baskets`, 2021-07-04) — One line + a meme: "Some people make babby. I make
  computer." Pure [[persona]].
- **Individual Sovereignty** (`individual-sovereignty`, 2021-07-11) — "Culture is downstream from
  technology." Sovereignty is a *technology* problem; recurrent expense is the trap; ecromata —
  "abolish scarcity." [[power-centralization]].
- **Consumption** (`consumption`, 2021-08-06) — Creation vs consumption (adulthood vs childhood);
  "in the absence of agendas, truth is the default." Advertising/finance create nothing.
- **Coliving Spaces** (`coliving-spaces`, 2021-08-10) — Short ask: looking for a high-competence
  group house. ("My first ask to this blog.")
- **The New Regime** (`the-new-regime`, 2021-09-23) — Refuses the NYT; "I do not respect the New
  York Times. They have no status or power to convey to me." Will follow whoever rebuts the
  Unabomber Manifesto. [[government-and-society]].
- **Class Warfare** (`class-warfare`, 2021-09-28) — The coffee-shop call; defines the
  professional managerial class as a parasitic, zero-sum majority; "grow the pie." Core
  [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]] text.
- **To Fight against Entropy** (`to-fight-against-entropy`, 2021-10-24) — Shelves the ecromata
  land project (sub-1 "management coefficient" without robots); the dream waits for AI. Robotic
  plumbers, robot-fixers. [[power-centralization]], [[comma-ai]].
- **An Architecture for Life** (`an-architecture-for-life`, 2021-10-29) — The MuZero
  representation/dynamics/prediction decomposition applied to comma; "watch us build a brain,
  watch us build life." "There's no such thing as consciousness, we are all p-zombies."
  [[ai-and-agi]], [[free-will-and-the-soul]].
- **On The University of Austin** (`on-the-university-of-austin`, 2021-11-09) — The UATX founders
  are "supporters of Power or naive political children"; predicts it gets a DEI dept in 10 years.
  To disrupt universities you must "also build a religion." [[government-and-society]].
- **A Correction on my AI chip thoughts** (`a-correction-on-ai-chips`, 2021-12-12) — Publicly
  reverses: VLIW & fancy compilers are *right* for ML (NNs are DSPs; same ops every run; Rice's
  theorem doesn't apply). "I'm kicking myself for getting this wrong." Model of his honesty.
  [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **The Fourth Estate** (`the-fourth-estate`, 2021-12-18) — The captured press; the "regime" =
  revolving-door same-people. Imagines an uncaptured research machine documenting the power
  structure. "The world has nothing I want… I want to live forever." [[government-and-society]].
