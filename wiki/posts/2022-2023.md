# Post summaries — 2022–2023 (exit, the tiny corp, no FOOM)

## 2022

- **Consumer Class Divide** (`consumer-class-divide`, 2022-02-02) — "I'd like to abolish the
  consumer class." Argues against consumption-as-moral-identity (Pepsi Generation). "Advertising
  should be illegal." [[wireheading-and-moloch]].
- **The Problem of the State** (`the-problem-of-the-state`, 2022-02-09) — "Social power is power
  over nature; state power is power over man." Exit > voice; replace the state's functions with
  locally owned machines. "Nothing… is worth the eternal boot." [[government-and-society]].
- **Brain FLOPS** (`brain-flops`, 2022-02-17) — Recomputes brain compute from neuron-firing
  energy: maybe only ~16 TFLOPS effective. "Maybe a 3090 is already a human brain." [[ai-and-agi]].
- **Beauty after COVID** (`beauty-after-covid`, 2022-03-05) — Cloth masks as religious symbol;
  Moloch; "Are you the problem?" stream poll. "Exit is probably the best we can hope for." Why
  he stopped streaming (first pass). [[wireheading-and-moloch]].
- **Vampire Attack Twitter** (`vampire-attack-twitter`, 2022-04-16) — A 5-step crypto "vampire
  attack" to clone Twitter onto a blockchain. HN-doubter jab: they can't admit anything's
  possible or they'd have to ask why *they* didn't do it. [[persona]].
- **On Device Learning** (`on-device-learning`, 2022-09-03) — AGI = "a machine capable of taking
  (almost) any human job." Must learn on-device (gravel, snow, banana peels). "What is the human
  reward function?" [[ai-and-agi]], [[comma-ai]].
- **Happy Birthday to Me** (`happy-brithday-to-me`, 2022-10-02) — 33rd birthday. "You are a bunch
  of randomly initialized dot products." The four AI labs framing. AlphaZero "relearned all of
  chess culture"; "so it shall be for human culture." [[free-will-and-the-soul]].
- **A Hope** (`a-hope`, 2022-10-13) — Patent-troll suit; rereads the Unabomber Manifesto. The
  hope: AI is *data*-limited (data replicates cheaply) not compute-limited → decentralization
  possible. "A world of open source personally owned decentralized AI." [[technology-and-freedom]].
- **The Hero's Journey** (`the-heroes-journey`, 2022-10-29) — Steps away from comma ("no longer a
  race car, it's a boat"). First names "the Tiny Corporation" (calls it a hobby). Seeks a literal
  hero's journey / Garden of Eden. [[timeline]], [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **Avatar 2 was Bad** (`avatar-2-was-bad`, 2022-12-27) — Rewrites the whole plot ("Titanic
  Edition"). Shows his creative/fiction streak and "denunciation of absolutes." [[persona]].
- **e/acc** (`eacc`, 2022-12-31) — "Acceleration is the greatest form of charity." Elon's power =
  "a new theory of management" (chaos prevents process). "What should the loss function be for
  organizations?" A real DAO with AI as the evaluator. [[economics-and-value]].

## 2023

- **Wokeism** (`wokeism`, 2023-02-13) — Reduces wokeism to one factually-false core belief
  (opportunity → outcome). "Factually false beliefs bind people better than true ones."
  [[government-and-society]].
- **Wireheading City** (`wireheading-city`, 2023-04-11) — A scaled Black Rock City for opting out
  of society (free food/shelter/drugs, leave anytime). Less cruel than prison; fully opt-in.
  [[wireheading-and-moloch]], [[three-class-society]].
- **A Person of Compute** (`a-person-of-compute`, 2023-04-26) — Defines 1 "person" = 20 PFLOPS;
  Tampa/Humanity units; 2038 as his singularity guess (Unix rollover). "Ugh this needs to happen
  sooner." [[ai-and-agi]].
- **the tiny corp raised $5.1M** (`the-tiny-corp-raised-5M`, 2023-05-24) — The founding manifesto.
  "Commoditize the petaflop." Start with software (AMD stack), not silicon. "If NVIDIA is the
  Apple, we are the Android." [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **A dive into the AMD driver workflow** (`a-dive-into-amds-drivers`, 2023-06-07) — AMD responds
  to his ROCm rants; deep dive on amdgpu vs Pro. "Let's make the driver developed in the open!
  …play to win, not just play not to lose." [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **A disgusting playbook** (`a-disgusting-playbook-copy`, 2023-07-20) — The startup-scam playbook
  (Cruise/Zoox): "the company is your product," sell first/build later. "I would rather lose than
  sell out… eat out of dumpsters [to] know the truth." Value-destruction metric. [[the-pmc-and-rent-seeking]].
- **choose your own adventure** (`choose-your-own-adventure`, 2023-07-23) — "Humanity does not
  have a common destiny!" Space/metaverse are big and expanding. "The future will really be yours
  to choose." [[ai-and-agi]].
- **A Really Big Computer** (`a-really-big-computer`, 2023-08-08) — Designs a GPT-4-in-a-day
  machine: wafer-scale compute + solar (skip the grid). "tiny corp's next raise will be $400M…
  No joke." [[tinygrad-and-tiny-corp]].
- **There is no hard takeoff** (`there-is-no-hard-takeoff`, 2023-08-10) — Key essay: the universe
  isn't Go (it contains the other computers). No FOOM unless someone gets a 51% attack on compute;
  compute distributes as a power law. "Let the markets cook." [[ai-and-agi]].
- **p(doom)** (`p-doom`, 2023-08-16) — Post-Yudkowsky-debate. Crux: formal systems vs inscrutable
  weight matrices (he says the latter, forever). One-shot prisoner's dilemma is unsolvable for
  real agents. Economy doubling every 2 years "sounds awesome." [[ai-and-agi]].
- **Three Class Society** (`three-class-society`, 2023-10-03) — Citizen / Subject / Incarcerated.
  "You should be able to choose to opt in or out, but you can't do both." Net-positive immigration
  rule; Tytler on democracy's collapse. [[government-and-society]].
- **Disrupt the Government** (`disrupt-the-government`, 2023-11-04) — Provide 10x-better
  decentralized versions of all eight government services until it withers. Money/energy/
  protection/education/healthcare playbook. "Can you build a high-trust decentralized society?"
  [[government-and-society]].
- **Imitation Learning** (`imitation-learning`, 2023-11-18) — comma's technical lineage:
  steering-angle → lane-correction → e2e; lanes were "the original sin." The billion-dollar
  question: how to end-to-end ground-truth the error-correction function. [[comma-ai]].
